Welcome to Bards Crossing

Bards Crossing was developed by Wensmann Homes Inc between 2003 and 2005 and is intended to be a community for persons 55 years of age or older. This luxurious 55+ community consists of a three-story condo building with a total of 55 units. Units range in size from approximately 830 to 1,520 square feet and have one or two bedrooms and one or two bathrooms. The building includes underground parking and elevator access. Some condo units feature updated kitchens, stainless steel appliances, spacious master suites, walk-in closets, private deck or balcony, and storage space.
